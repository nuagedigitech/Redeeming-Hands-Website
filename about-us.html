About Us

When supporting us, we fight together to survive.

REDEEMING HANDS Cares for you, established in 2013 by Mrs. Evelyn Stephen with a noble vision to address the needs of families with special children born with autism, ADHD intellectual disabilities and other developmental disabilities.

Our Services

  • Organizing free medical camps once in three months for General Health checkup, Eye care and dental care for the children with special needs, street children, semi-orphans and orphans.
  • Education support for rural children through evening tuition centers.
  • Providing Hands on training on Income generation Economic activities among SELF HELP Groups of women to improve their socio-economic status.
  • We help the parents of the Special children for sustainable Livelihood and conducting training programs on the skills they are interested E.g.: ( Tailoring, Jute bags making, artificial jewelry making,  and other handicrafts works )
  • On Festive occasion’s distribution of clothes, gifts, diapers and their daily needs are provided from the sponsors to the children with low economic status.
  • We also provide guidance and support to parents of these special children nearly 25 families.
  • We make these children feel wanted and loved.
