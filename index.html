Redeeming Hands Cares for you is a nonprofit organization founded in the year 2013. Evelyn Stephen started working with a noble vision to uplift the deprived, the destitute, street children, rural women and to eradicate poverty through various economic programs, child health, child education and women empowerment. We also believe in extending a hand in times of natural calamities and also strive to bring holistic development in the society. Learn More

Our Vision

To bring a change in the underprivileged people and to leap boundaries and to create a name for themselves in this competitive society.
