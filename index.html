About Us

When supporting us, we fight together to survive.

REDEEMING HANDS Cares for you, established in 2013 by Mrs. Evelyn Stephen with a noble vision to address the needs of families with special children born with autism, ADHD intellectual disabilities and other developmental disabilities.

Our Services

  • Organizing free medical camps once in three months for General Health checkup, Eye care and dental care for the children with special needs, street children, semi-orphans and orphans.
  • Education support for rural children through evening tuition centers.
  • Providing Hands on training on Income generation Economic activities among SELF HELP Groups of women to improve their socio-economic status.
  • We help the parents of the Special children for sustainable Livelihood and conducting training programs on the skills they are interested E.g.: ( Tailoring, Jute bags making, artificial jewelry making, and other handicrafts works )
  • On Festive occasion’s distribution of clothes, gifts, diapers and their daily needs are provided from the sponsors to the children with low economic status.
  • We also provide guidance and support to parents of these special children nearly 25 families.
  • We make these children feel wanted and loved.

YOU CAN MAKE A DIFFERENCE...

When our little children are overwhelmed by big emotions- It’s our job to share our care, support and to protect the children.

We have children who Scream, Run around or even throw objects in public as they have Sensory overload, Frustrated for little things and they can’t verbalize their emotions.

We are in need of medication support to every child as the cost is quite high. If you feel responsible to do your bit to improve the lives of these children, you may set apart a small amount of money monthly, or even make a one-time contribution to the cause.

Donors are welcome to sponsor in part/Full (the cost of medication /daily needs) you can also support a family and differently abled children (financial assistance). You may request a periodical update of child’s progress and celebrate your birthday/ special occasion with the children.

Individual/Institutional donor referrals are welcome and shall be acknowledged suitably.

Her smile is gracefully charming. Her eyes speaks pages of her burning desires, struggles to live a peaceful life, if not plentiful riches. In a world of wasted wishes and dusty dreams let ours be the hand that always reaches for the children with special needs.

Autism is a lifelong developmental disability that affects how a person communicates with and relates to others.


All these kids need is a little hope, a little help and someone who believes in them. It is our privilege to fight for the child needs and extend our support by reaching you…
